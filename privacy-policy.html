Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 1, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Quit Bad Habits ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

  • Profile Data: Name, profile photo (optional), and motivation preferences
  • Habit Data: Habit names, categories, progress tracking, completion records
  • Usage Statistics: App usage patterns, feature engagement, success rates

1.2 Device Information

  • Device identifiers for crash reporting and analytics
  • iOS version and device model for compatibility
  • App version and performance metrics

1.3 Notification Data

  • Notification preferences and scheduling data
  • Push notification tokens (stored securely)

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 Core App Functionality

  • Track your habit progress and provide personalized insights
  • Generate statistics and milestone celebrations
  • Send motivational notifications and reminders
  • Calculate savings (money and time) from breaking bad habits

2.2 Premium Features

  • Generate detailed PDF progress reports
  • Provide unlimited habit tracking
  • Offer advanced analytics and insights

2.3 App Improvement

  • Analyze usage patterns to improve user experience
  • Fix bugs and optimize performance
  • Develop new features based on user needs

3. Data Storage and Security

Local Storage First: Your habit data is primarily stored locally on your device using Core Data, ensuring your privacy and data control.

3.1 Local Storage

  • All habit tracking data is stored locally on your device
  • No habit details are transmitted to external servers
  • You maintain complete control over your personal data

3.2 iCloud Sync (Premium Feature)

  • Optional iCloud synchronization for premium users
  • Data encrypted in transit and at rest using Apple's security
  • Only habit data synchronized, no personal identifiers

3.3 Security Measures

  • Industry-standard encryption for all data transmission
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • No third-party access to personal habit data
  • Secure authentication for premium features

4. Third-Party Services

4.1 RevenueCat (Subscription Management)

  • Purpose: Process premium subscriptions and manage billing
  • Data Shared: Anonymous user identifier, purchase information
  • Privacy Policy: RevenueCat Privacy Policy

4.2 Apple App Store

  • Purpose: App distribution and in-app purchases
  • Data Shared: Purchase records, app usage analytics (anonymized)
  • Privacy Policy: Apple Privacy Policy

5. Your Privacy Rights

5.1 Data Access and Control

  • Access: View all your habit data within the app
  • Export: Export your progress data via PDF reports (Premium)
  • Delete: Delete individual habits or all data through app settings
  • Correction: Edit or update your habit information at any time

5.2 Notification Control

  • Complete control over notification preferences
  • Disable notifications through iOS Settings or in-app controls
  • Customize notification timing and frequency

5.3 Premium Subscription Control

  • Cancel subscriptions through Apple App Store
  • Data retention continues locally after cancellation
  • Premium features become unavailable after subscription ends

6. Children's Privacy

Our app is designed for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately.

7. International Users

Our app is available globally. If you use our app outside of Turkey, your information may be transferred to and processed in Turkey or other countries where our service providers operate. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for such transfers.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by:

  • Posting the updated policy in the app
  • Sending a push notification about policy changes
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy

9. Data Retention

  • Habit Data: Stored locally until manually deleted by user
  • Account Data: Deleted within 30 days of account deletion request
  • Analytics Data: Anonymized and retained for up to 2 years for app improvement
  • Purchase Records: Retained as required by Apple App Store policies

10. Your Consent

By using Quit Bad Habits, you consent to the collection and use of your information as described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with this policy, please do not use our app.
